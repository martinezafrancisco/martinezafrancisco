About Me

I grew up never understanding why things were the way they were (you can ask my mother about that!). Whenever I think about why I pursued journalism and storytelling as a career, I often come back to that thought I kept asking myself. In such a complex world, I wanted to try to understand why it's complex and how it got to that point. That's what I aspire to aim for in my work.

Outside of it, I'm a fan of sports, music and a novice darts player (read: terrible).
